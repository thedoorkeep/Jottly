What is Jottly?

Collaboration at Your Fingertips

Jottly is a place to bring your ideas and let them grow. Writers, editors, idea makers, content creators, and more can find someone to help take their thoughts and turn them into something more. If you need a really great writer to flesh out your idea, you can find that here. If you need an editor to elevate your project to the next level, you can find that here.

Access to Thousands of Other Creators.

Jottly is a two-sided marketplace where they can find real people that they don’t already know to work with. While once a publisher was needed to find talented people, now you have Jottly

Unlimited Storage

Never let a concept go to waste. Jottly is an infinite notebook to hold all your ideas.

Community Based Collaboration

Jottly has thousands of users, each with unique ideas and talents. Meet new content creators at the click of a button and let your ideas grow.

Who Uses Jottly?

Jottly has a user base of thousands of writers, editors, journalists, musicians, poets, and more all sharing their ideas and thoughts. If you're looking to collaborate with a type of creative, you'll find them here.
